Change Your Backdrop

Decor items set the backdrop for your Home.

1. Choose Me -> My Home to jump home from anywhere. Try it now. There you are in your new home!

2. Choose Catalog -> Decor to look for something cool.

3. Browse through some of the many player-made decor options. Click one you like and buy it!

4. To use your new decor, choose My Stuff -> Decor and click the the little house next to your new home decor.

5. Click the close box to close your inventory and see your new room full size. Redecorating made easy!

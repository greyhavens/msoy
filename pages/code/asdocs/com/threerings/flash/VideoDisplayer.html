_videoCheckerproperty 
protected var _videoChecker:Timer

Checks the video every 100ms to see if the dimensions are now know. Yes, this is how to do it. We could trigger on ENTER_FRAME, but then we may not know the dimensions unless we're added on the display list, and we want this to work in the general case.
